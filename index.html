From the baker

Floretlye began from a quiet wish: to make everyday life feel a little gentler.

These madeleines are baked in small batches with flavours inspired by warm teas, soft evenings and slow mornings. Each box is meant to be a pause in your day – a reminder to breathe, slow down, and treat your heart kindly.

FAQ

How long do the madeleines last?

As they’re freshly baked with no preservatives, they’re best enjoyed within 3 days at room temperature. To keep them fresh for up to 7 days, store them in the fridge in an airtight container.

Where do I collect my order?

Self-collection is available at Serangoon MRT (Exit G), or at your preferred MRT station with a small additional fee. Full details will be sent in your confirmation email.
